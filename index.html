About Us

Attendees will learn from the experiences of experts and professionals in Law, Finance, and Engineering among other industries. Attendees will also partake in a number of workshops throughout the day, each giving an in-depth view of the work in the selected career focus.

What is Youth Achieving Success?

Youth Achieving Success (YAS) is a student-run non-profit organization dedicated to assisting students on the path to post-secondary education. YAS strives to prepare students by providing financial aid and knowledge on sought-after industries through an annual career conference to educate students on the variety of career options available to them.

What is the YAS Career Conference?

The YAS Career Conference is a one-day event that brings seven different industry professionals to one location. High school and university students have a chance to attend all seven workshops in addition to an opening speech conducted by an esteemed keynote speaker. Students get a chance to learn from and speak with unique professionals from different career fields while networking with other like-minded youth. All YAS Career Conference attendees have a chance to apply for the YAS Scholarship which will be awarded after the conference.

Is there a fee to attend YAS?

Since YAS will be held at a prime location and will be offering one deserving student a scholarship, the conference will require a very low fee of $20 from its attendees to cover venue costs and other expenses.

What kind of career fields will be at this event?

Our executives have strived to have professionals from a variety of sought-after careers to attend the event. The seven fields at YAS 2015 are Web Development, Finance, Social Entrepreneurship, Law, Marketing, Medicine, and Policing.
