This Year At PacificMUN

Pacific Model United Nations 2017 is the newest hotel conference in the Lower Mainland, providing delegates with another opportunity to engage themselves in world affairs. Many students across the Lower Mainland have found themselves unable to attend Model United Nations conferences because of the steep price. PacificMUN has placed a priority on being affordable and is proud to say that it is the most affordable hotel Model United Nations conference in North America.


Our conference would also hope delegates gain valuable experiance at this years conference, whether it be insight about a global issue that many nations are facing, or something about themselves as a person and how they interact with people. Pacific Model United Nations is a positive learning environment for delegates to explore their interests with our vast selection of committees and topics.

Aurora


Aurora - /əˈrôrə,ôˈrôrə/ - noun

1. a natural electrical phenomenon characterized by the appearance of streamers of reddish or greenish light in the sky.

2. the dawn.

In the past, socials at Model United Nations conferences were intended to allow delegates to meet, network, and have fun with each other in a non-professional setting, but somewhere along the way, we feel that this original intention got left behind. We hope to bring that back again with our signature gala social, Aurora. Compared to the socials that other Model United Nations conferences have, Aurora is PacificMUN’s bold take on a classic idea.

Taking place on the 19th floor of the Pinnacle in the Vistas 360 room, our social will include panoramic views of Downtown Vancouver, the North Shore, and Vancouver Harbour as the Vista rotates once every hour. A black-tie event, this social will be supplied with copious amounts of hors d'oeuvres, desserts, and (non-alcoholic) drinks that should make delegates enjoy our social that much more.

As part of one of our three pillars, PacificMUN hopes to provide a place where delegates can spark friendships, meet like-minded individuals, and make connections that will last a lifetime. We hope that Aurora will be the beginning of a new dawn of socials, and with a dazzling display of photos, food, and fun, Aurora is an event you don’t want to miss!

With restaurants of all different tastes to choose from just minutes away, the cuisine around the Pinnacle hotel will be sure to satisfy any and all cravings.

Conference Policies

PacificMUN has established several conference policies in order to ensure the safety of all delegates, staff and other conference participants. Our policies are as follows:

1) Delegates are expected to attend all committee sessions, unless they have been excused by their head delegate or sponsor teacher, and have notified their committee staff by email, explaining their reason for absence.

2) Delegates are to remain respectful of all individuals during the conference, including staff, sponsor teachers, Secretariat members and other delegates.

3) All individuals at PacificMUN are required to wear conference-issued name tags for identification and security reasons; Individuals may be required to present their name tags in order to enter certain conference events or committee sessions.

4) If delegates have any medical conditions that may impact their participation during the conference, they are required to notify their delegation’s primary contact (either a sponsor teacher or head delegate)

5) PacificMUN maintains a zero tolerance policy towards any and all actions, objects, materials and substances that are deemed as illegal or illegal in the presence of minors by the Government of Canada, the Province of British Columbia, the City of Vancouver, and any other municipal bodies or authorities. a. Such items may include but are not limited to: weapons, drugs, alcohol and any illegal substances or substances illegal in the presence of minors; b. If your child is caught in possession of, using, or caught in the presence of peers using the illegal substance, you will be notified immediately, and you will be required to retrieve your child from the conference premises. Your child will not be allowed on conference premises for the remainder of the conference.

6) Vandals assume all responsibility of structural damage done to the conference venue, including but not restricted to, the Pinnacle Harbourfront Vancouver Hotel.

7) Delegates will abide by signage and restrictions set forth by the conference facilities, including but not limited to, the Pinnacle Harbourfront Vancouver Hotel.

8) A firm curfew will be implemented for delegates by the conference between the hours of 11:59PM and 6:30AM, during which delegates will be required to stay in their assigned rooms, unless involved in a committee session that is run and supervised by PacificMUN.

9) Co-educational rooming is prohibited at any time during the conference.

10) All violations of the above policies will be resolved on a case-by-case basis by the Secretariat of PacificMUN 2017. PacificMUN reserves the right to suspend individuals for a specific duration from their role as delegate at the conference and: a. Expulsion of the individual from the conference and it’s premises will occur if appropriate;
