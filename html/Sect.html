His fourth year being involved in Model United Nations, having attended, staffed, and been on Secretariat teams at numerous conferences in the past, he thoroughly enjoys every single moment of it.

Christopher Yuen is a student entering Grade 12 at Vancouver College, and feels humbled to be serving as the Deputy Secretary-General: External at PacificMUN 2017. Joining Model United Nations in Grade 10, it has fostered many opportunities for Christopher, as well as deepened his understanding in world politics and international affairs. Charles Balcita is a Grade 12 student at Johnston Heights Secondary and is ecstatic to serve as the Under Secretary-General of Committees for PacificMUN 2017! Discovering the world of Model United Nations in Grade 9, Charles had no idea what he was going into as a young student or how important MUN would be in his life. Serving previously as a delegate, staff and Secretariat member, Charles is honoured to be one of the USGs of Committees to introduce the Model UN community to new and exciting committees as well as interesting and significant topics relevant to our modern lifestyles.

Other than Model United Nations, Charles can often be found furiously planning out club meetings at school, working with different non-profits, or going around Surrey and Vancouver with friends. Throughout much of Charles' MUN career he has met many individuals who he is very proud to call his friends. Of all the things that Charles values in MUN, he has found the networking aspect of every debate the best because of all the opportunities that it can bring to virtually all of the students who fully participate. Charles invites all delegates new to Model United Nations or those curious about why their friends dress up in suits 4 times a year to attend PacificMUN 2017! The staff and Secretariat team have been working hard to cater this experience to all delegates, and he looks forward to meeting all delegates!
