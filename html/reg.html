Registration Policy

Registration Instructions

If you are an Independant delegate, please indicate this in the drop down menu when asked to provide your school. If you are affiliated with a school, please indicate this on this on the form. If you not listed in the drop down menu, please fill out the delegation registration form. Please note that only sponsor teachers and head delegates may fill out the form, and please ensure that the contact information submitted in the form is reliable, as it will be the conference’s primary contact point for the delegation.

Delegation Registration

In order for your delegation to be added to the list, your school must first fill out the delegate registration form. Within 48 hours of you filling out the form, your school’s name will appear in the drop down menu on the delegate registration form. After this is complete, delegates may be registered for the conference with your delegation. Delegation assignments and rooming assignments will be sent to the delegation’s primary contact via a shared spreadsheet.

Day Delegate Registration

Delegates who wish to participate in the conference, but who do not require a room at the Pinnacle Harbourfront Hotel may wish to register as Day Delegates for a reduced price. If this is the case, please fill out our Day Delegate Registration form. Please note that Day Delegates are not affiliated with a delegation, unless specified prior to registration. If your delegation has a delegate who does not require a room, please have your primary contact request this in an email, before filling out the form.

Sponsor Teachers and Chaperones

Sponsor teachers may be the primary point of contact between the conference and the school. In this case, all requests and any other relevant information about the delegation will be required of them, and all information about the conference will be sent directly to them.

Head Delegates

Delegations will be required to have a head delegate as a secondary point of contact. The Head Delegate may sometimes be the primary point of contact for a delegation, as per the information given in the delegation registration form. Head Delegates also have the responsibility of attending various meetings throughout the conference weekend.

Rooming Policies

At PacificMUN 2017, we will not be taking rooming requests from Independent delegates. If an independant delegate has a rooming requirement, or would like to express a concern about this, they may contact our USG of Delegate Affairs via email.

Delegations may submit their delegate rooming preferences through email when prompted by our USG of Delegate Affairs. Shortly thereafter, our team will submit a confirmed list of rooms to the delegation. Please note, at PacificMUN 2017, we will only be providing single-gendered rooms.

Financial Aid

If you would like to apply for financial aid, please fill out our financial aid form first, then allow our team to process your application. Pending the results, our USG of Delegate Affairs, will email you a new link for you to register yourself. Our subsidized amount ranges from 50% - 100%. If you have applied to financial aid, please wait for our USG of Delegate Affairs to reach out to you prior to filling out any more registration documents.

Refund Policy

Please note our refund policy. If you would like to request a refund, please contact our USG of Delegate Affairs.

75% refund until November 20, 2016

50% refund until December 20, 2016

25% refund until January 10th, 2017

No refund available after January 10, 2017
